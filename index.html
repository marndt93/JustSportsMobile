About Just Sports

We are a sport organization that strives to improve the health of individuals by providing excellent sport camps for all ages whether they are a child, teenager, or adult. There are multiple sports that are avaliable.

Our goal is to meet the needs of our community in a fun and exciting way. To allow people to play sports throughout the year and to bring the community together in a healthy and slightly competitive way.

Sports that are Avaliable

Soccer One of our most popular sports camps. We have 3 different leagues; minor, teenager, adult. These sport camps run from June 1st - September 28 on Thursdays at 6:00pm
Baseball This is also a very popular sport camp. These camps run on Tuesdays at 6:00pm and run from June 20th - August 29th. The teams consist of 15 people
Flag Football Football camps are on Saturday morning 9:00am starting June 3rd and ends September 2nd
Basketball Basketball camps are both winter and summer camps. Summer camps are on Wednesdays at 6:00pm and start on June 7th and ends August 30th
Volleyball Teams consist of 10 people. These camps run on Monday nights at 6:00pm and starts June 5th and ends August 28th
Hockey One of our most popular winter sports camps. There are camps from all ages two different times per winter. Dates are to be announced in September
